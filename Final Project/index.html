Our Mission

Birds of Paradise Sanctuary & Rescue is dedicated to advocating for captive exotic birds and providing a permanent, loving home for the parrots in our care.

Through education, rescue, and sanctuary, we work to give each bird a life filled with safety, enrichment, and respect.

Why Your Help Matters

Caring for rescued parrots is a lifelong commitment. Many of the birds at Birds of Paradise Sanctuary & Rescue cannot be adopted out and will spend the rest of their lives at the sanctuary.

Your support helps provide:

  • Fresh food and clean water every single day
  • Veterinary care for chronic health issues and emergencies
  • Enrichment and toys that keep intelligent birds mentally active
  • Safe aviaries and perches designed for climbing and flying

Every sponsorship, donation, and share of our mission makes a direct difference in the quality of life for the parrots who depend on us.
