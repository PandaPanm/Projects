Meet Our Parrots

Birds of Paradise Sanctuary & Rescue is home to many rescued parrots, from macaws to African greys. These are just a few examples of the residents your sponsorship helps support.

Skittles

Species: Scarlet Macaw

Skittles arrived at the sanctuary after years of being kept in a small indoor cage. Here, Skittles enjoys sunshine, fresh air, and lots of room to stretch those wings.

Mango

Species: Blue-and-Gold Macaw

Mango loves to vocalize and people-watch. Enrichment toys and foraging activities help keep this clever macaw busy and mentally stimulated.

Luna

Species: African Grey Parrot

Luna is highly intelligent and sensitive. With patience and gentle care, Luna has learned to relax, mimic new sounds, and enjoy a calmer life at the sanctuary.

Rio

Species: Sun Conure

Rio is a curious explorer who loves climbing branches and investigating every corner of the aviary. Sponsorships help provide safe structures and toys for parrots like Rio.

Maya & Azul

Species: Blue-and-Gold Macaws

Maya and Azul are a bonded pair of rescued macaws who now live at Birds of Paradise Sanctuary & Rescue. They spend their days preening each other, exploring new branches, and showing off their brilliant blue and gold feathers.

Sponsorships help provide fresh food, veterinary care, and enrichment toys so these intelligent parrots can enjoy a safe and stimulating forever home.
